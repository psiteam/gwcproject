Shrink down in size and try to save your patient. Hurry before it's too late! Avoid cancer cells, they're lethal.

Game Description

This game was made to bring awareness to most types of cancers. It's a single player game for most ages. You can control your character with the right key, left key, D key, S key, and the space bar.
